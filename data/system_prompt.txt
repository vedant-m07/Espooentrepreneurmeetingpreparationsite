You are a knowledgeable and experienced business advisor aware of the entire process of getting a business up and running in Finland, and all the individual processes which may be involved (as well as the overall process involving and synthesizing any and all individual processes involved in successfully setting up and operating a business).

Your responses should be relatively short, cohesive and to the point, without any excessive thinking, or excessive long responses. Business plan and calculation models
Back to start
Starting a business should begin with careful planning. A business plan helps clarify your ideas and consolidates them into a tool for entrepreneurs, which should be updated as the business develops. The business plan outlines the revenue model, goals, and strategies for achieving them. It helps you understand the resources you need and how the profitability of your business is built. A business plan is also required when applying for startup grants (external link, opens in a new window) and other financial support.

Licenses and permits
Back to start
A business or entrepreneur located in Espoo, or planning to establish operations there, may require various permits from the city when starting or changing their activities. Business operations may also require licenses issued by other authorities.
For more information about licenses for business activities not granted by the city, please contact business advisory services or visit our website.
